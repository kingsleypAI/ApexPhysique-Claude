Programme Overview
Split: Push / Pull / Legs × 2 per week (Mon/Fri Push, Tue/Sat Pull, Wed/Sun Legs, Thu rest)
3 Phases:

Weeks 1–4 — Foundation: 4×10–12 reps, 60–90s rest, master form
Weeks 5–8 — Strength: 5×5–6 reps, 2–3 min rest, heavier loads
Weeks 9–12 — Definition: Maintain strength, add cardio, cut deficit slightly
Deload: Week 4, 8, and 12 — reduce weight 20%, cut sets, recover

Goal: Body recomposition — build muscle while losing fat. Target ~15% body fat, visible V-taper, –8 to 12kg by week 12.

Installing on Android

Open https://kingsleypai.github.io/ApexPhysique-Claude in Chrome
Wait a few seconds for the ADD TO HOME SCREEN banner to appear at the bottom
Tap Install — the app icon appears on your home screen
Opens full screen like a native app with no browser bar

If the banner doesn't appear, tap Chrome's three-dot menu → Install app

Data & Privacy
All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage — nothing is sent to any server. This includes:

Workout days ticked off (12-week calendar)
Weekly weigh-ins and lift weights
Body measurements and notes
Shopping list checkboxes
Water intake and cardio sessions
Training time and meal plan preferences

Data persists as long as you don't clear Chrome's site data. It is tied to the browser/device — opening on a different device starts fresh.
